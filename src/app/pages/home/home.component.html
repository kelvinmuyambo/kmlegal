About Us

Kamusasa & Musendo Legal Practitioners is a registered private legal firm registered in terms of the Legal Practitioners Act (Chapter 27:07) and with the Law Society of Zimbabwe. It consists of a team of efficient, astute, visionary professionals and principled legal practitioners and supporting staff with vast experience in the legal field who devote their energies in pursuit of their calling into the practice of Law and its execution.

The legal firm undertakes work in most legal disciplines such as contract law, insurance law, construction law, criminal law, banking law, civil litigation, criminal litigation, constitutional advice and litigation, conveyancing and notarial work etcetera. We also offer efficient debts recovery services.

The firm has a clear cut mission tailor-made to meet the needs of individual and corporate clients on a person to person basis. We strive to accommodate diverse clients notwithstanding the size be they individuals, corporate, associations, clubs, churches, nongovernmental organizations, quasi-government organizations etc. We provide a complete package of professional services under one roof.
